Journey Into Tech

Why Tech?

Since I was younger, I have always had an affinity for science and technology. To me, technology has been able to accomplish things that would otherwise, be considered magic. The ability to control my home simply by speaking out loud is something I wish I could go back and tell my younger self about. I am often one of the first adopters of new products and features because my curiosity about the subject is never-ending. I often dream about what the world of tech will be in 50 or 60 years.

LaunchCode

Why LaunchCode?

I heard about LaunchCode and subsequently LC101 when I discovered Harvard's CS50 class when it popped up on my YouTube suggestions and have been curious about coding ever since. I have spent most of my working life in health care and the service industry and I was looking for a change that I can hopefully spend the rest of my life doing it.
